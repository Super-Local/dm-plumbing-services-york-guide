Our Plumbing Services in York

DM Plumbing Services covers a wide range of residential, commercial and emergency plumbing work across York and the surrounding villages. Below is a straightforward guide to what we offer.

24/7 Emergency Plumber

We're available around the clock, every day of the year. Whether it's a burst pipe in the middle of the night or a serious leak on a bank holiday, you can call us and we'll get to you as quickly as possible. There are no hidden call-out charges for emergency repairs.

Emergency Plumbing Repairs

When something goes wrong and you need it fixed fast, we prioritise getting to you and resolving the problem properly. Common emergency jobs include burst or frozen pipes, sudden water pressure loss, and overflowing toilets or drains. We serve properties right across the York area, from the city centre to outlying villages like Tadcaster and Hemingbrough.

Leak Repairs

Undetected leaks can cause significant damp damage and lead to costly repairs if left too long. We locate and fix leaks at their source, whether that's a pipe joint, a fitting, or a connection behind a wall. Early action saves money and protects your property.

Broken Tap Repairs

A dripping or broken tap wastes water and can be surprisingly disruptive. We repair and replace taps in kitchens and bathrooms, working with all common tap types and fittings. It's a straightforward job we can usually complete in a single visit.

Radiator Leak Repairs

A leaking radiator can damage floors and skirting boards and reduce your heating system's efficiency. We identify the source of the leak – whether it's a valve, joint or the radiator body itself – and carry out a lasting repair.

Blocked Drains

Slow or blocked drains in sinks, baths and toilets are one of the most common plumbing problems we deal with across York. We clear blockages quickly and can advise on preventing them recurring, particularly useful given the limescale build-up common in North Yorkshire's hard-water areas.

Bathroom Emergencies

From overflowing baths and broken shower fittings to failed seals and cracked pipework, we handle a full range of urgent bathroom plumbing issues. We carry common parts and fittings so we can resolve most problems on the first visit.

Kitchen Emergencies

Leaking pipes under the sink, a faulty dishwasher connection or a blocked kitchen drain can quickly cause water damage to cabinets and flooring. We respond promptly to kitchen plumbing emergencies and make sure everything is properly secured before we leave.

Plumbing and Heating

We cover general plumbing and central heating work, including fault diagnosis on boilers and heating systems, hot water problems, and water pressure issues. For gas appliance and boiler work, a Gas Safe registered engineer is required by law – please ask us and we'll point you in the right direction.

Residential and Commercial Plumbing

We work in both domestic homes and commercial premises across York and the surrounding area. Whatever the scale of the job, we bring the same straightforward approach: clear pricing, qualified tradespeople, and work guaranteed for 12 months.

Property Maintenance

Ongoing property maintenance keeps small issues from becoming expensive ones. We're happy to carry out routine plumbing checks, minor repairs and maintenance visits for homeowners, landlords and managing agents throughout the York area.
