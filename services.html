Plumbing services in York and the surrounding areas

DM Plumbing Services covers a broad range of residential, commercial, and emergency plumbing work across York. Below is a straightforward overview of what we do.

24/7 emergency plumbing

Plumbing crises — burst pipes, sudden leaks, loss of hot water — can happen at any hour. We offer a genuine 24-hour emergency plumber service in York with no hidden call-out charges, so you know exactly where you stand when you ring us. We aim to reach you as quickly as possible, day or night.

Leak repairs

A slow leak can cause serious damp and structural damage if it's left unattended. Our team carries out thorough leak detection and repair work on pipes, joints, and fittings throughout the property. Early action is always cheaper than dealing with the consequences later.

Broken tap repairs

A dripping or seized tap wastes water and can be surprisingly disruptive. We repair and replace taps of all types in kitchens and bathrooms, including older fittings that can be tricky to source parts for. Most tap repairs are completed in a single visit.

Radiator leak repairs

Leaking or cold radiators are a common problem in York homes, particularly as central-heating systems age. We locate and fix radiator leaks, bleed systems, and resolve issues with valves and fittings to keep your heating running efficiently. This kind of work also helps protect floors and walls from water damage.

Blocked drains, sinks, and toilets

Blocked drains are one of the most frequent plumbing calls we receive across the York area. We clear blockages in sinks, toilets, baths, and external drains quickly and cleanly. Limescale build-up — common in North Yorkshire — can contribute to recurring blockages, and we can advise on longer-term solutions.

Bathroom and kitchen emergencies

From a cracked toilet cistern to a kitchen pipe that's come away under the sink, we handle urgent bathroom and kitchen plumbing problems as part of our emergency and same-day service. We carry a range of common parts and fittings so most issues can be resolved on the first visit.

Plumbing and heating

We cover broader plumbing and heating work including hot water cylinder issues, water pressure problems, and central-heating faults. If your property needs a Gas Safe registered engineer for boiler or gas appliance work specifically, we'll let you know clearly — we don't carry out gas work outside our qualifications.

Residential and commercial plumbing

DM Plumbing Services works with both homeowners and commercial clients across York and the surrounding villages. Whether it's routine maintenance for a landlord's property or a more involved plumbing project for a business, we offer free no-obligation quotes and the same 12-month workmanship guarantee on every job.

Property maintenance plumbing

Keeping a property in good order often means staying on top of smaller plumbing tasks before they become bigger ones. We carry out ongoing property maintenance plumbing for landlords, letting agents, and homeowners who want a reliable local contact they can call on when something needs attention.
